Clear Cache & Refresh

If you're not seeing your services or latest updates, click the button below to clear your browser cache and reload the app.

Manual Steps (if button doesn't work):

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac)
  2. Or press F12 to open console, then type:
    localStorage.clear()
    and press Enter, then reload the page
