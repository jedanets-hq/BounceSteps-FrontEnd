# 🔧 HARAKA - Jinsi ya Kuona Services na Providers

## Tatizo
- Database ina services 2 na providers 5
- Browser inaonyesha providers 2 tu na services 0

## Suluhisho la Haraka

### Hatua 1: Clear Browser Cache
1. **Fungua browser** (Chrome/Edge/Firefox)
2. **Bonyeza:** `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`
3. **Chagua:** "Cached images and files"
4. **Bonyeza:** "Clear data"

### Hatua 2: Hard Refresh
1. **Nenda:** http://localhost:4028
2. **Bonyeza:** `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows) au `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac)
3. **Au bonyeza:** `Ctrl + F5`

### Hatua 3: Angalia Console
1. **Bonyeza:** `F12` (kufungua Developer Tools)
2. **Nenda:** Console tab
3. **Angalia:** Kama kuna errors

## Kama Bado Haifanyi Kazi

### Test Backend Moja kwa Moja
Fungua browser tab mpya na nenda:
- **Services:** http://localhost:5000/api/services
- **Providers:** http://localhost:5000/api/providers

Unapaswa kuona JSON data na services 2 na providers 5.
